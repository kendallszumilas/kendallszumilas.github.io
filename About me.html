Hey I'm Kendall. I began photographing when I was 13.
My parents bought me a small Canon Powershot and that started the
passion. I'm 20 years old and am currently based out of Orono, ME.
I carefully choose locations based on golden hour lighting. Sun
flares and bright, warm tones are my signature. Please visit the "Contact" page so I can
answer any questions you may have!
